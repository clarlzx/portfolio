    As part of the animation and modeling class, students learnt about the techniques employed for animation (i.e. keyframes, forward & inverse kinematics, etc.) and for modeling (i.e. mesh modification, reconstruction, etc.). In this animation assignment, students were tasked with making a short animation, following the whole process of the animation pipeline. This animation was completed in a team of 3 (big thanks to my teammates Xingjian and Andy), using Unreal Engine 5 and Blender.
